This led me to investigating autonomous surveying tools and procedures that would greatly improve the productivity and efficiency of an earthwork project. There has been a large increase in using 3D laser scanning and GPS technology to compute volumes of previously surveyed land for earthwork. Said technologies can be used along side government provided contours of a surveying site and still implement known 2D methods, formulas and procedures.
